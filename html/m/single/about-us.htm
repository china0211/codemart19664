关于我们

Company Profile

Maxsun Industries Incorporated was established in 2002,is a leading supplier of nutritional raw materials.Maxsun is dedicated to continuously improvement, reliable testing and reliable quality assurance. 


Maxsun is contracted with 9 organic growers to bring you the best organic raw materials.In 2014, Maxsun’s organic production lines were certified by the USDA Organic.There are over 50 kinds of organic products, including organic grasses, organic herbs and organic algea.


Products&Technologies

The product of maxsun covers Organic Protein Powder、Fruit Juice Powder、Fruit Extract、Herb Ingredients、Workout、Nootropic、Cosmetic Ingredients、Medical Intermediate、Natural Sweetener、Amino Acid.Maxsun’s team provides customers with valuable formulation services, excellent customer service and contract manufacturing options.


Production control

Qualified products need the excelsior pofessional attitude to qulity target. Before the production process, we will point out the quality target and producing requirement, also we will monitor and trace and check the process to guarantee the process be recorded in detail,  so that the traceability and quality consistency can be guaranteed. As for the final products, the marking, carrying, package, storage and other links will be supervised and defended strictly. We treat every producing link with our rigorous attitude to strive for perfection.



Production test

As for the testing process, except our R&D room and equipped testing facilities, we also have good  relationship with a number of well-known domestic and foreign testing centers. We mainly test the products naturalactive ingredients, pesticide  residue, residual  solvent, heavy metals and microorganism strictly. From raw material purchase --- producing process --final products, we all have strict control of the quality.

Quality and customers are number one priority to Maxsun, we will strive to offer high quality products to customers.
